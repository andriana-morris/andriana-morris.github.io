Walt Disney World

The Happiest Place on Earth


Walt Disney World is my absolute favorite vacation spot. We made our first visit when I was in middle school and I love it now just as much as I did back then! There is no better place to embrace your inner child and have a blast. The ambiance is magical, the service is spectacular and the food is delicious! I recommend a trip for vacationers of all ages!
